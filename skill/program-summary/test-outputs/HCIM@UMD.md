## 学费与奖学金

- **学费（州内居民）**：约 $37,000（36 学分总计）（[官网](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/)，2025 年数据）
- **学费（州外/国际生）**：约 $72,000（36 学分总计）（[官网](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/)，2025 年数据）
- **奖学金**：项目不提供机构奖学金，学生主要通过自费、贷款或勤工俭学解决学费。约 1/3 的学生可在校内获得 Graduate Assistantship（每周 10-20 小时，部分包含学费减免）。（[官网 FAQ](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/frequently-asked-questions/)）

## 申请要求

- **截止日期**：2026 年 1 月 6 日（Fall 2026 入学）；每年仅录取一届，秋季入学，无春季入学。（[官网](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/graduate-admissions/)，⚠️ 请以最新官网为准）
- **TOEFL / IELTS**：
  - iBT TOEFL 96+（口语 22，听力 24，阅读 26，写作 24）
  - IELTS Academic 7.0+（听力 7，阅读 7，写作 7，口语 6.5）
  - PTE Academic 68+
  - （[官网](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/graduate-admissions/)）
- **GRE**：可选，非必须（参考分数：Verbal 40 百分位，Quantitative 60 百分位）（[官网](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/graduate-admissions/)）
- **推荐信**：3 封，来自导师、雇主或同事（不可为家庭成员）（[官网](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/graduate-admissions/)）
- **GPA**：本科及研究生阶段绩点不低于 3.0/4.0（[官网](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/graduate-admissions/)）
- **作品集（Portfolio）**：必须提交，3-5 件作品样本（可接受单件高质量作品），内容涵盖设计/艺术、社会科学、技术等方向。可提交线框图、UX 报告、用户旅程地图、代码样本、案例分析等，需为非加密 PDF 或公开链接。（[官网 FAQ](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/frequently-asked-questions/)）
- **其他材料**：目的陈述（Statement of Purpose）、简历/CV、HCIM 补充申请文件（回答 6 选 3 问题）、$75 申请费（[官网](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/graduate-admissions/)）

## 课程与项目特色

- **核心课程**：3 门核心课 + 研究方法课 + 实习/论文/Capstone + 3 门选修课，共 30 学分（[官网](https://academiccatalog.umd.edu/graduate/programs/human-computer-interaction-hcim/human-computer-interaction-ms/)）
- **第一年典型课程**：《编程导论》《HCI 基础》《用户体验研究方法》（[官网 FAQ](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/frequently-asked-questions/)）
- **两条毕业轨道**：
  - **Capstone Track**：通过 iConsultancy 与业界客户合作完成全周期设计项目（[官网](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/)）
  - **Thesis Track**：由论文导师和委员会指导的学术研究论文（[官网](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/)）
- **世界级 HCI 实验室**：学生可接触 UMD Human-Computer Interaction Lab (HCIL)，该实验室为世界顶级 HCI 研究机构（[官网](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/)）
- **跨学科背景**：整合信息学、计算机科学、教育、心理学与工程，不要求有编程基础（[官网](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/)）
- **400+ 合作机构**：与业界和政府机构的广泛合作网络（[官网](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/)）
- **上课形式**：核心课程需到校上课，无纯在线选项；建议配备 16GB RAM 以上专业笔记本（[官网 FAQ](https://ischool.umd.edu/academics/masters-programs/master-of-science-in-human-computer-interaction/frequently-asked-questions/)）
