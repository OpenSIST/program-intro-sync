## 申请要求

- **截止日期**：Fall 入学 — Early Action 12 月 1 日；境外国际生最终截止 6 月 1 日；境内国际生 7 月 1 日；本地申请者 8 月 1 日。Spring 入学 — 国际生（境外）11 月 1 日，其余 12 月 1 日（[官网](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msis-information-systems/master-of-science-in-information-systems-boston/)，⚠️ 请以最新官网为准）
- **TOEFL / IELTS**：TOEFL iBT 最低 79 分；IELTS 最低 6.5；Duolingo 105（[官网](https://coe.northeastern.edu/academics-experiential-learning/graduate-school-of-engineering/graduate-admissions/)）
- **GRE**：2025–2026 学年免提交（[官网](https://coe.northeastern.edu/academics-experiential-learning/graduate-school-of-engineering/graduate-admissions/)）；[CSGrad](https://github.com/csms-apply/csgrad/blob/main/docs/B-/NEU%20IS.md) 亦确认 2025-2026 申请季无需 GRE
- **推荐信**：2 封（[官网](https://graduate.northeastern.edu/programs/msis-information-systems/master-of-science-in-information-systems-boston/)）

## 课程与项目特色

项目共 32 学分，核心必修课为 INFO 5100/5101（Application Engineering and Development，4 学分）。完成方式可选纯课程（Coursework）、课程+项目（Project）或论文（Thesis）三种路径。（[官网 Academic Catalog](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/engineering/multidisciplinary/information-systems-msis/)）

**方向与专业化**（[官网](https://coe.northeastern.edu/academics-experiential-learning/academic-departments/seis/academics/ms-insy/)）：
- AI Systems Engineering
- Business Intelligence and Data Analytics
- Full-Stack Software Engineering
- Machine Learning and Data Sciences Engineering
- Project / Product Manager
- User Experience Design
- Medical Software Engineering（可选 Concentration）

**Co-op**：可选，最长 8 个月全职实习，合作雇主超 3000 家（含 Google、Amazon、Goldman Sachs、Tesla、Apple 等）。（[官网](https://coe.northeastern.edu/academics-experiential-learning/academic-departments/seis/academics/ms-insy/)）

课程涵盖加密货币工程、大数据系统、网络安全、机器学习、用户体验设计、商业智能分析等。（[官网 Academic Catalog](https://catalog.northeastern.edu/graduate/engineering/multidisciplinary/information-systems-msis/)）
